About This Car

The Maruti Swift is the ultimate choice for practical urban mobility. Known for its peppy performance and exceptional fuel efficiency, it navigates congested city streets with ease. Powered by a refined 1.2L DualJet petrol engine producing 89 PS and 113 Nm of torque, the Swift delivers a spirited driving experience while returning an impressive fuel economy. Its lightweight yet rigid platform ensures agile handling and a composed ride. Inside, the cabin features a smartplay infotainment system, automatic climate control, and a well-appointed dashboard with premium materials. Safety highlights include dual airbags, ABS with EBD, and a high-strength body structure designed to protect occupants.
